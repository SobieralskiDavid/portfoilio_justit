Building design

Branding

Branding and brand management are some of the most important aspects of building an interactive and recognizable online presence. It is important to create a strong first impression on browsers of your site, if people enjoy the look and layout they are more likely to become immersed in the content and buy into your brand.

Responsivity

With the growing habits of mobile and tablet users your site needs to be ready for any style of browsing, building for mobile first and scaling up to the biggest of displays will ensure your message will come across no matter the place or time of view, putting convenience and accessibility into every hand.

Colour coordination

The choice of colors on your website are an important branding tool, they allow the viewer to gain a familiarity with your organization, with the color scheme reflecting throughout your site it can show a coordinated message that gives off a strong impression.

Content flow

The flow of content and the users interaction with your site can decide on whether or not a person decides to continue browsing, the journey through your site should tell a story that explains the brands philosophy and clearly explains the services offered.

Navigation

One of the most important elements of any site is the ability of the viewer to navigate to different parts and services without having to spend time searching, the links should be plainly marked and be in a coherent order, they should in essence be stepping stones on the browsing journey.

In the Loop, In the Link

The proliferation of social media and the viewing habits of potential customers makes for a crucial part of your brands reach, all socials avenues should be clearly shown, linking to your profiles is a necessary way to extend your message in different ways, tailoring them to the demographic of the audience.
