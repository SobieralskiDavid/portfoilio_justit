Why learn a language ?

I have always had a fascination with language, the flow of words, the manipulation of meaning and the phonetic distillation of sense and sentiment; it seems then only natural that this fascination would extend to computer languages, and it does,it's an extra way to converse with reality and decide what creations can be perceived.

What did it all start

The first computer I ever had was a Sinclair ZXSpectrum 48k, it was fully black with a rainbow flash of colour on the right hand side representing the refracted light of a prism, the games would load with a screeching noise and so many flashing lights that today it would come with a warning, I had been introduced to Computers, a silent interest had begun.
